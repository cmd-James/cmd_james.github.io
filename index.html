Capstone Project

BEng Electrical Engineering — University of Pretoria

Final Year Project · Power Systems Major
1 kW Multilevel Inverter for HVDC Systems
Designed, built, and deployed a multilevel inverter for a High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) system — including full PCB hardware construction and execution of a control algorithm on an embedded processing platform. The core challenge was designing a 1 kW inverter capable of powering regular household devices using lower-voltage semiconductor switches, requiring innovative multilevel topology selection and precise gate-drive control to achieve the required output voltage and waveform quality.
  • Hardware Design: Full PCB layout and construction from schematic to physical board.
  • Control Algorithm: Implemented and validated a switching control algorithm on an embedded DSP platform.
  • Power Electronics: Applied multilevel inverter topology to achieve high-voltage output from low-voltage switches.
  • System Validation: Tested and verified the inverter powering real household loads at rated output.
